§ 01 — About

About the Group

The Wai Wong Research Group is based in the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at the University of Canterbury. The group works on transportation engineering problems related to big data analytics, connected and autonomous vehicles, traffic flow theory, signal optimization, intelligent transportation systems, emergency response and evacuation, and sustainable transport.
