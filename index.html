About Us

The Wai Wong Research Group conducts research on transportation engineering, traffic flow theory, intelligent transportation systems, connected and automated vehicles, and transport safety.

Our work combines mathematical modelling, simulation, data-driven analysis, and empirical validation to understand and improve the performance, reliability, and safety of transportation systems.

Research Areas

  • Traffic flow theory and modelling
  • Connected and automated vehicles
  • Signalized intersection operations
  • Traffic safety and human factors
  • Data-driven transportation systems
